Come with us!

NTU Open Source Society is one of the leading tech communities at Nanyang Technological University. We hold weekly events, where we invite members of the student community or industry professionals to conduct workshops, talks and/or networking events in order to equip our members with industry-relevant skills and provide exposure to the tech world outside of NTU. Traditionally, these events have been held on Friday evenings and have been appropriately called TGIFHacks!
